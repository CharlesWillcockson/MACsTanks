About us

I'm Marcello A. Cantu , the owner of M.A.C.s Tanks. (Marcellos' Aquariums Corals & Tanks) I specialize in saltwater but can suit any aquarium needs. I sell and service aquariums and equipment. I have saltwater fish and coral for sale. My goal is to set up aquariums for people at an affordable price using proven methods I've tried at my own home in my personal tanks. I want people to have something that they can be proud of as well as to take the hassle out of having to worry about the health of their animals.
